Mad Axles: Beyond Bumperchrome

Welcome to Beyond Bumperchrome. No idea where we got that name from. Pretty simple life-and-death game here. No pressure.

Pick a robot model (give it a nickname if you want). It will come with a weapon, but you also get to attach a second weapon on it. Weapons will increase damage but can also slow down your speed/accuracy if they are heavy.

Then you must add a random bonus item. They can help make up for any shortcomings your robot may have at this point. Maybe. At this point, it's all a toss-up.

After all that constructing and hours spent agonizing over every screw and bolt, you will fight your opponent in the Bumperchrome Arena. Each weapon has limited energy, so hope that all your attacks hit their mark or deal enough damage to demolish your enemy before you run out of juice.

If both robots lose all their weapon power before their health runs out, judges will pick the bot with the most health as the winner. Good luck. You'll need it. Seriously, skill means nothing here but you can try. It's all mostly luck.
